Setting objectives - Promoting Customer lifetime value (CLTV)

• Promote cohort retention: the cost of maintaining old customers is only 1/5 of the cost of acquiring new customers.

• Focus on customer lifetime value, as long as customer retention rate with an increase of 5% can enhance the enterprise by 25%~95% profit.
